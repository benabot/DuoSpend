# Privacy Policy — DuoSpend

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## Summary

DuoSpend does not collect any personal data. Your data stays on your iPhone. No information is transmitted to external servers, third parties, or the app's developer.

---

## 1. Data collected

DuoSpend does not collect any personally identifiable information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:
- projects you create (name, emoji, budget, partner names);
- expenses you record (title, amount, date, payer, split ratio).

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted, analyzed, or sold.

---

## 2. Local storage

All data is stored on your iPhone using SwiftData, Apple's persistence framework. It remains on your device until you uninstall the app or delete your data from the Settings screen.

---

## 3. App Group (widgets)

When you use the home screen widgets, a limited copy of your data is shared between the app and the widget extension via a local App Group. This mechanism is managed by iOS and does not involve any external data transfer.

---

## 4. In-app purchases

If you purchase DuoSpend Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. DuoSpend does not receive or store your payment information. Only the purchase state (unlocked or not) is stored locally on your device.

---

## 5. Tracking and advertising

DuoSpend contains:
- no tracking or analytics systems;
- no advertising SDKs;
- no collection of identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) for targeting purposes;
- no third-party integrations (Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.).

---

## 6. iCloud sync

Version 1.0 of DuoSpend works entirely offline. No iCloud synchronization is active. If a future version introduces synchronization, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

---

## 7. User rights

You can delete all your data at any time from **Settings → Data → Delete all data** in the app. This action is irreversible.

---

## 8. Children

DuoSpend is not intended for children under 13. No data relating to minors is collected.
